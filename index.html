Why This Works

CS:GO skins are finite. Valve retired CS:GO in September 2023. Legacy skins are frozen in time. No more will ever be created.
Trade-ups destroy supply daily. Players burn 10 lower-tier skins to craft 1 higher-tier skin. Supply permanently shrinks every single day.
Accounts go dark forever. Bans, lost passwords, abandoned accounts. Millions of skins are locked in accounts that will never trade again.
CS2 brought new demand. A new generation of players wants legacy skins, but there are fewer available every month.
Automation gives us the edge. While others manually browse listings, our bot buys 24/7. Claude spots underpriced skins instantly. x402 settles payments in milliseconds.

FAQ

Do you already have a massive inventory?
We're in active collection mode. The stockpile grows every day as our bot runs 24/7. Check our Steam inventory for the current state.
How does the AI decide what to buy?
Claude analyzes every listing against our criteria: price relative to market average, float value, collection gaps, and rarity. If it passes, the buy executes instantly.
What is x402?
x402 is a payment API that enables instant, automated transactions. We use it to fund Steam Market purchases without manual intervention or wallet management.
What if Valve floods the market?
Valve has never re-released retired skins or capsules. Their economy depends on scarcity. Re-releasing items would destroy trust in the entire Steam marketplace.
